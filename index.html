Professional Services

As a dedicated GIS specialist and consultant with extensive expertise in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), remote sensing, vulnerable smart agriculture, ecosystem conservation, and geospatial modelling, I deliver data-driven solutions for sustainable environmental and agricultural challenges. Proficient in tools such as ArcGIS, QGIS, ENVI, Google Earth Engine, MaxEnt, and InVEST, I specialize in satellite imagery processing, land-use/land-cover mapping, spatial analytics, precision farming with IoT integration, crop health monitoring, climate-resilient agricultural models, habitat fragmentation simulations, biodiversity assessments, and carbon sequestration modelling. My consultancy experience spans collaborations with governments, NGOs, and private sectors on projects including disaster response planning, protected area design, soil and water resource management, and policy-informed restoration strategies, transforming complex geospatial data into actionable insights that promote resilience, ecological balance, and innovation.
